3 Tips on getting the Best Sound Quality while recording:

  • Do not touch the Mic once the recording Starts
  • Make the room as quiet as possible – close windows, silence notifications, close vents, etc
  • Don't eat the mic. A trick is to extend your thumb and pinkie (hang loose), touch one end to your chin and the other to the mic, and that is the distance you should keep for the whole interview.
